# LOGICAL OPERATORS

## AND OPERATOR
  - It is use it when we have to satisfying both the given condition.

  | C1 | C2 | R |
  |----|----|---|
  |T   | T  |  T|
  |T   | F  |  F|
  |F   | T  |  F|
  |F   | F  |  F|


#### 1. WAQTD THE DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEE EARNING SAL MORE THAN 2000 AND WORKING IN DEPT 10
    SELECT *
    FROM EMP WHERE SAL>2000 AND DEPTNO=10;

#### 2. WAQTD THE DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEES WORKING AS A MANAGER IN DEPT 20 AND EARNING SAL MORE THAN 1500     
    SELECT *
    FROM EMP 
    WHERE JOB='MANAGER' AND DEPTNO=20 AND SAL>1500;

## OR OPERATOR
 - It is used it when we have to satisfied either of the given condition.


 | C1 | C2 | R |
 |----|----|---|
 T    |T   |T
 T    |F   |T
 F    |T   |T
 F    |F   |F  
 

#### 1. WAQTD THE DETAILS OF EMPLOYEE WORKING AS A MANAGER OR CLERK
    SELECT * 
    FROM EMP WHERE JOB='MANAGER' OR JOB='CLERK'

#### 2. WAQTD THE DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEE WORKING IN DEPTNO 10 OR 20 OR 30
    SELECT *
    FROM EMP WHERE DEPTNO=10 OR DEPTNO=20 OR DEPTNO=30

#### 3. WAQTD THE DETAILS OF THE EMPLOYEE WORKING AS MANAGER AND CLERK
    SELECT *
    FROM EMP WHERE JOB='MANAGER' AND JOB='CLERK';

## NOT (NEGATIVE OPERATOR)

- It is `unary` operator which is use it to negate the given condition.

|C1  | R  |
|----|----|
| T  | F  |
| F  | T  |
